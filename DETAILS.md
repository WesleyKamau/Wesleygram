WesleyGram

This project was put together in 5 days, right before my birthday, December 23rd.

I had this idea in the shower, "What if everyone's profile photo was about me?" I had made a powerpoint presentation a few weeks ago where I'd recreated some friends' profile photos in Minecraft (Which was really fun) and I kind of wanted to stem off of that.

All of the people who either followed or were followed by me, @wesleykamau, on 12/17/2025, has had their profile picture "Wesley-ified". Hence, Wesleygram. Rolls off the tongue, right?

I had a lot of fun making this, and I learned a lot in the process. I even collaborated a little bit, @dehliaferrante on the logo. I hope you find this cool!
